Fashion and Design Technology

Duration: Diploma – 2 Years | Certificate – 1 Year
Awarding Body: Commission for Technical and Vocational Education and Training (CTVET)
The Diploma in Fashion and Design Technology merges creativity with technical skill, preparing students for success in the fashion industry. Learners gain expertise in pattern drafting, garment construction, textile design, and clothing business management. They also participate in fashion shows and workshops, gaining hands-on experience in turning design concepts into finished products. This course nurtures entrepreneurial and artistic talent, enabling graduates to launch their own fashion brands or work professionally in fashion houses.
The Certificate in Fashion and Design Technology provides beginners with the fundamental skills of sewing, tailoring, and fabric selection. Over one year, students learn the essentials of garment making and design, allowing them to create high-quality clothing for clients or small businesses. It serves as an excellent introduction to fashion design, with the option to progress into the Diploma level for advanced specialization.

Accounting

Duration: Diploma – 2 Years | Certificate – 1 Year
Awarding Body: Commission for Technical and Vocational Education and Training (CTVET)
The Diploma in Accounting provides in-depth training in financial management, auditing, taxation, and bookkeeping. Students learn to interpret financial data, prepare reports, and make sound business decisions. The course emphasizes accuracy, integrity, and analytical thinking — qualities essential for anyone managing or supporting financial operations. Graduates can pursue careers as accountants, finance officers, or tax administrators, or continue their studies in advanced accounting programmes.
The Certificate in Accounting focuses on the foundational aspects of accounting and finance. Students are introduced to bookkeeping, record management, and basic financial reporting. This one-year programme is perfect for those looking to begin a career in business finance or as a stepping stone toward the Diploma qualification.

Business Administration

Duration: Diploma – 2 Years | Certificate – 1 Year
Awarding Body: Commission for Technical and Vocational Education and Training (CTVET)
The Diploma in Business Administration develops a strong foundation in management, communication, finance, and entrepreneurship. Students learn how organizations function and how to apply effective business strategies to achieve success. The programme focuses on marketing, human resource management, accounting, and leadership, preparing students for administrative and supervisory roles in both public and private sectors. It is ideal for learners who aspire to become business leaders or entrepreneurs managing their own ventures.
The Certificate in Business Administration offers a fast-track introduction to business operations and management principles. Over one year, students learn the basics of communication, office procedures, record keeping, and customer service. It serves as an excellent starting point for those seeking immediate employment or planning to advance into the Diploma programme later.

Public Relations

Duration: Diploma – 2 Years | Certificate – 1 Year
Awarding Body: Commission for Technical and Vocational Education and Training (CTVET)
The Diploma in Public Relations equips students with the skills to manage and promote the image of organizations effectively. It trains learners in communication strategy, media relations, event management, and digital public communication. Through practical projects, students learn to handle press releases, manage campaigns, and build positive relationships between companies and their audiences. Graduates are well-prepared to work as public relations officers, brand ambassadors, or communication specialists in various industries.
The Certificate in Public Relations introduces students to the fundamentals of professional communication. The one-year course focuses on event coordination, media writing, and basic PR tools used in corporate and social settings. It is ideal for beginners who wish to gain practical knowledge in public engagement and marketing communication, or for those who plan to pursue advanced studies in Public Relations at the Diploma level.

Information & Communication Technology

Duration: Diploma – 2 Years | Certificate – 1 Year
Awarding Body: Commission for Technical and Vocational Education and Training (CTVET)
The Diploma in Information & Communication Technology prepares students to excel in today’s digital economy. Learners gain practical experience in computer hardware, software, web development, and networking. The programme emphasizes technology as a tool for business growth, innovation, and entrepreneurship. Students graduate with the competence to work as IT support specialists, system administrators, or web developers, or to establish their own ICT-based businesses.

Students receive hands-on training in basic computer operations, internet use, and data management — essential skills for the modern workplace. This one-year course provides a solid base for entry-level IT positions or progression into the Diploma in ICT.

Graduates leave prepared to meet the growing demand for skilled beauty and cosmetology experts in Ghana and beyond.

Academic Program Structure

Explore the flexible structure of our academic programs designed to suit different schedules and goals.

Our Regular Programmes run from Monday to Friday, - 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM providing students with a full academic experience that combines theory, practical training, and continuous assessment. These weekday sessions offer an immersive learning environment where students engage deeply with their coursework, participate in hands-on projects, and receive close guidance from instructors. The structured schedule ensures consistent learning progress, personal development, and mastery of both academic and technical skills essential for career success.

Our Weekend Programmes are specially designed for working professionals and busy individuals who wish to further their education without disrupting their weekday schedules. Classes are held on Saturdays - 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM and Sundays - 12:30 PM to 5:00 PM, offering a convenient and flexible learning option. Despite the compact timetable, the quality of instruction and academic support remains the same as our regular programmes. Students benefit from engaging lectures, practical sessions, and collaborative learning experiences that fit seamlessly into their personal and professional lives.

At Mighty Eagles College, our programmes are designed to fit your goals and schedule, offering practical and flexible learning options for all students.

  • Certificate Courses (3–6 Months) : Short-term, skill-focused programmes offering practical knowledge for quick employment or business startup. Ideal for learners who want to gain new skills within a shorter period.
