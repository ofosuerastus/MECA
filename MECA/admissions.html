Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Applicants:
Applicants must possess passes in six (6) subjects (3 core and 3 electives) at the WASSCE/SSSCE level. Core subjects must include English Language, Mathematics, and Integrated Science or Social Studies.

Mature Applicants:
Candidates must be 25 years and above with at least three (3) years of relevant work experience. They will be required to pass an entrance examination and attend an interview.

International Applicants:
Applicants must have equivalent qualifications to the WASSCE or other recognized credentials. They must also possess a valid passport and meet language proficiency requirements.
